💡 Exam Tips

The ECO is the exam blueprint. Every question maps to a domain, task, and enabler. Study to the ECO, not the PMBOK.

Enablers are illustrative, not exhaustive. The exam may test concepts beyond the listed enablers.

People is the largest domain (42%). Don't underestimate it — servant leadership, conflict resolution, and stakeholder engagement dominate.

~50% of questions involve agile/hybrid — spread across all three domains, not just Process.
