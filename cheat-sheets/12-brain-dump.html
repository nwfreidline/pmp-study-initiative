  • 3. Smooth/Accommodate — Emphasize agreement
  • 4. Force/Direct — Emergency only

👥 Tuckman's Stages

  • Forming → Polite, uncertain (PM: Direct)
  • Storming → Conflict, power struggles (PM: Coach)
  • Norming → Trust, cohesion (PM: Support)
  • Performing → High productivity (PM: Delegate)
  • Adjourning → Closure (PM: Celebrate)

📝 Contract Types (Buyer Risk → Seller Risk)

  • CPPC — Cost + % of cost (worst for buyer)
  • CPFF — Cost + fixed fee
  • CPIF — Cost + incentive fee
  • T&M — Time & materials (shared)
  • FPIF — Fixed price + incentive (PTA applies)
  • FFP — Firm fixed price (worst for seller)

📋 Change Control Process

  • 1. Change identified
  • 2. Assess impact (scope, schedule, cost, risk)
  • 3. Submit change request
  • 4. CCB reviews (approve/reject/defer)
  • 5. Update plans & baselines if approved
  • 6. Communicate decision
NEVER make the change immediately. ALWAYS assess first.

🧠 PMI Mindset Quick Rules

  • Servant leadership > authority
  • Assess before acting (always)
  • Follow the process (always)
  • Never gold plate
  • Ethics first (honesty, responsibility, respect, fairness)
  • Engage stakeholders early & continuously
  • Charter = PM's authority
  • Facilitate > Direct
