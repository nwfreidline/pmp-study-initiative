# PMP Study Plan — Revised (Practice-First Approach)

**Target exam date**: ~June 2026 (before July 9 ECO change)
**Time available**: ~8 weeks
**Strategy**: ECO-driven, practice-heavy, PMBOK as reference only

## Guiding Principles
- The ECO (Exam Content Outline) is the blueprint — study to it, not the PMBOK
- 1,000+ practice questions is the #1 predictor of passing
- ~50% of the exam is agile/hybrid — don't neglect it
- Understand the PMI mindset: servant leadership, process compliance, assess before acting
- Use the PMBOK only to look up specific concepts you don't understand

## Recommended Materials
See `curated-study-resources.md` for full details. Minimum viable stack:
- Andrew Ramdayal Udemy course (~$15 on sale) + YouTube
- PMI Study Hall Essential ($49)
- TIA Exam Simulator (~$45)
- Your ECO PDF (already in this folder)

## 8-Week Schedule

### Week 1: Foundation + ECO Deep Read
- [ ] Read the Exam Content Outline PDF thoroughly — annotate it
- [ ] Watch Andrew Ramdayal's PMI mindset overview videos (YouTube, free)
- [ ] Skim PMBOK 7 principles chapter (Ch 3) — just the 12 principles
- [ ] Take a diagnostic quiz (50 questions) to find your baseline

### Week 2: People Domain (42% of exam)
- [ ] Servant leadership model
- [ ] Conflict resolution (collaborate, compromise, smooth, force, withdraw)
- [ ] Tuckman's model (forming, storming, norming, performing, adjourning)
- [ ] Emotional intelligence, motivation theories (Maslow, Herzberg, McGregor)
- [ ] Stakeholder engagement strategies
- [ ] Complete 150 People-domain practice questions
- [ ] Review every wrong answer — understand WHY

### Week 3: Process Domain Part 1 (50% of exam)
- [ ] Integration management, project charter, change control
- [ ] Scope management, WBS, requirements traceability
- [ ] Schedule management: critical path, PERT, crashing, fast-tracking
- [ ] Cost management: estimating techniques, budgeting
- [ ] Memorize ALL EVM formulas (see key-formulas.md)
- [ ] Complete 150 Process-domain practice questions

### Week 4: Process Domain Part 2
- [ ] Quality management (7 basic tools, cost of quality, PDCA)
- [ ] Risk management (qualitative/quantitative, response strategies)
- [ ] Procurement (contract types: FFP, FPIF, CPIF, CPAF, CPFF, T&M)
- [ ] Communications + resource management
- [ ] Complete 150 more Process-domain practice questions

### Week 5: Agile & Hybrid Deep Dive
- [ ] Scrum framework (roles, events, artifacts)
- [ ] Kanban, XP, Lean basics
- [ ] Agile metrics (velocity, burn-down/up, cycle time, lead time)
- [ ] Hybrid approaches: when to use predictive vs agile vs hybrid
- [ ] User stories (INVEST criteria), Definition of Done
- [ ] Watch David McLachlan's 200 Agile Questions video (YouTube, free)
- [ ] Complete 150 agile-focused practice questions

### Week 6: Business Environment + Integration
- [ ] Benefits realization and value delivery
- [ ] Organizational change management
- [ ] Compliance and regulatory considerations
- [ ] Review all three domains holistically
- [ ] Complete 100 mixed-domain practice questions
- [ ] Take FULL-LENGTH practice exam #1 (180 questions, timed, no breaks cheating)
- [ ] Score and review every wrong answer

### Week 7: Targeted Review + Practice Exams
- [ ] Analyze exam #1 results — identify top 5 weak areas
- [ ] Targeted study on weak areas only
- [ ] Take FULL-LENGTH practice exam #2
- [ ] Begin brain dump practice (write key formulas in < 5 minutes)
- [ ] Complete remaining practice questions to hit 1,000+ total

### Week 8: Final Prep
- [ ] Take FULL-LENGTH practice exam #3 (target: 75%+ on TIA, 65%+ on Study Hall)
- [ ] Brain dump practice daily
- [ ] Light review of flashcards and formulas only
- [ ] Last 2 days: NO studying. Rest, eat well, sleep well.
- [ ] Exam day logistics: valid ID, arrive early, take both breaks
