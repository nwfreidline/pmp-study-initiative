# Week 1: Foundation + ECO Deep Read

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this week, you should be able to:
1. Describe the PMP exam structure, format, scoring, and logistics
2. Explain the three ECO domains and their weightings
3. List and explain all 35 ECO tasks and understand what enablers are
4. Recite and explain the 12 PMBOK 7 principles
5. Describe the 8 PMBOK 7 performance domains and how they interact
6. Distinguish between predictive, adaptive, and hybrid delivery approaches
7. Articulate the PMI mindset and how it shapes exam answers
8. Complete a 50-question diagnostic quiz and identify your baseline
